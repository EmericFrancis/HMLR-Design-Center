Service Mapping

Tools to make service maps

  • pen and paper
  • post it's - often used for refining and questioning the map as a team
  • Sketch - for producing high quality printable outputs

Creating a map

Creating a service map can be a long process that requires constant itteration. If your planning on creating a service map make sure you have access to Subject matter experts relating to the process your trying to map. As quite often this will be the first time people are trying to document knowledge previously stored in the heads of experinced caseworkers or hidden 'cheat sheats/knowledge items'. To find out more read this GDS blog about service mapping.
